      1. Key generation

        1. \(p \in Z_P, q \in Z_P, p \ne q \)

        2. \(N = p \times q\)

        3. \(\phi(N) = (p-1) \times (q-1)\)

        4. Choose \(e\) when \(1 < e < \phi(N)\) and coprime with \( N, \phi(N)\)

        5. Choose \(d\) when \( (e \times d) mod \phi(N) = 1\)

        3. \( (d^k) mod (m) \)
